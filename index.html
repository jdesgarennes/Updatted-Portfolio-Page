About Me

My is John des Garennes, I am currently working as a network Engineer, and Learning web development. I am motivated to learn, and work hard at expanding my skillset to include Web development, and backend API infrastructure along with the concept of “Infrastructure as Code” Mentality. I Currently see the need to organize, and expand the management and creation of IT infrastructure, such as Network, Virtual systems, Storage, and Cloud infrastructure via source-controlled code. My goal is to be able to not only organize all aspects of this, but to make it controllable and organized, so that others do not need to be experts in every field, but will be able to make declarative change control of all aspects of the environment. Some of my other interests are Electronics, 3D printing and other DIY projects. I Primarily like to utilize my current and future skills with my hobbies and DIY projects. I feel it adds a lot of diversity to how I go about solving problems, and challenges at work and at home. I also take a great interest in science and the community at large. I currently live in Murrieta CA and work both at home and in the office for my employer. I work particularly well with groups and feel more productive when I am working with others.
